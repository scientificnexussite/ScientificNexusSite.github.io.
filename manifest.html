3. Community Features

Signed-in users (Google account required, free) have access to the following community features. These are all stored in Cloud Firestore.

FeatureHow It Works
User ProfileCustomisable username and avatar stored in Firestore. Visible to other users.
Social Hub (Chat)Real-time global and private chat built on Firestore's real-time listener system.
Ally SystemAdd other users as allies (friends). Ally requests are stored per-user in Firestore.
Private MessagingOne-to-one private chat with optional 4-digit PIN lock for privacy.
Global Feedback HubPublic review board. Any signed-in user can post a review, reply, like, or dislike entries.
BlacklistUsers can block specific other users from contacting them.

5. Platform Policies at a Glance

Data & Privacy

Only email, username, and avatar are stored. No data is sold to third parties. Full details in the Privacy Policy.

Age Requirement

The gaming platform is open to all ages. Users under 13 should have parental consent before creating an account, per COPPA guidelines.

Advertising

The platform uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Ads are served by Google and may be personalised based on cookies. Users can opt out via Google's ad settings.

User Conduct

Harassment, spam, or misuse of the community features will result in account suspension. Full terms in the Terms of Service.

Game Licensing

All games are sourced from GameDistribution under their standard publisher licensing terms. Scientific Nexus does not own the games themselves.

Account Deletion

Users can request complete deletion of their account and associated data by emailing scientificnexussite@gmail.com.
