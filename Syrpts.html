The Engineer Manifesto

Engineers spend months on sites with no return. The traditional model relies on donations, which are functionally one-way transactions where the supporter receives nothing of financial value in return.

Syrpts (SilverCash) restructures this paradigm. By creating a decentralized asset system, Syrpts allows creators to hold supply and investors to buy in.

When you back an engineer via Syrpts, you aren't just donating—you are acquiring an asset. It turns support into a growing asset for both creator and investor, aligning incentives and driving sustainable technological development.
