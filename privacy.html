Privacy Protocol (Privacy Policy)

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Information We Collect

Scientific Nexus operates as a high-fidelity research terminal. To facilitate this, we collect necessary telemetry and identity data via Firebase Authentication. When you link to the Nexus, we store your chosen username, assigned Commander ID, and basic profile metrics.

2. Firebase & Firestore Data Usage

Our database architecture strictly compartmentalizes your data:

  • User Profiles: You have full ownership of your profile data. External modifications to your ally (friend) arrays are heavily restricted.
  • Game & Simulation Stats: Your kinetic yields, levels, and XP are stored securely. Write access to these stats is strictly restricted to our secure administrative backend.
  • Communications: Chat data is encrypted and restricted solely to the verified sender and receiver.

3. Google AdSense & The DART Cookie

We use Google AdSense to fund our server uplinks. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.

  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Scientific Nexus respects global data privacy frameworks:

  • GDPR (European Union): You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data stored on our servers. You may export your profile dossier at any time.
  • CCPA (California): We do not sell your personal information. You have the right to request full disclosure of the data we collect and request its deletion.

5. Contact Command

For data removal requests or privacy inquiries, contact our secure channel at: scientificnexussite@gmail.com.
